About

Welcome to Discover Kozhikode, your ultimate guide to experiencing the vibrant charm of Kozhikode! From serene parks, majestic hills, and pristine beaches to historical landmarks, bustling malls, and ancient forts, we cover it all. Explore the city’s tranquil wetlands, sparkling rivers, picturesque ponds, and stunning waterfalls, or indulge in its diverse food spots, cozy cafes, and top-notch hotels. For those seeking entertainment, we highlight the best theatres, turfs, and cultural gems. Our mission is to showcase Kozhikode’s natural beauty, rich heritage, and modern attractions, ensuring every traveler has a memorable experience.
